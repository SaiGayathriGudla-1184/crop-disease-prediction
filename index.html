Problem Statement

Crop diseases can devastate yields, leading to significant financial losses for farmers. Early detection and timely intervention are crucial for effective management. Our AI-driven system analyzes crop images and environmental data to predict potential disease outbreaks, offering farmers actionable insights and treatment recommendations.

Expected Solution

Our mobile and web application uses machine learning algorithms to:

  • Identify crop diseases through image recognition.
  • Analyze real-time environmental data to predict disease outbreaks.
  • Provide farmers with tailored recommendations for preventive measures and treatments.

Empowering farmers with this cutting-edge technology helps protect crops and ensures better yields.

Key Features

  • AI-powered crop image recognition
  • Real-time environmental data integration
  • Push notifications for disease risk alerts
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Accessible via mobile app and web
